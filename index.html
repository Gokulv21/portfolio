  • Behavior Biometric Authentication using QKAE:
    • In this project, we combined quantum communication with biometric technology to create a revolutionary identity verification system. By leveraging the principles of quantum mechanics, particularly quantum entanglement, we developed an ultra-secure method for transmitting sensitive data. Our system captures unique behavioral patterns—such as typing speed and cursor movements—to build a user’s behavioral profile. This profile is then encrypted using the BB84 protocol, a quantum cryptography method that ensures the highest level of security. The integration of Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) with Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) adds an additional layer of protection, making it nearly impossible for hackers to breach the system. This technology has significant potential in sectors like government, finance, and healthcare, where the security of sensitive information is critical. Our work represents a step forward in cybersecurity, providing a robust, future-proof solution that adapts to the rapidly evolving technological landscape.
